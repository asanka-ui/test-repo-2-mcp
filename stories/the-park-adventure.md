# The Park Adventure

Max was a golden retriever with a heart as big as his appetite for tennis balls. Every morning, he would wait by the front door, tail wagging furiously, hoping today would be the day.

"Park time!" his owner Sarah finally announced one sunny Saturday.

Max bounded out the door and down the sidewalk, his ears flopping with each joyful leap. At the park, he spotted his best friend—a scrappy terrier named Biscuit.

The two dogs raced across the grass, chasing squirrels they'd never catch and digging holes they'd never finish. They splashed through the creek, rolled in questionable patches of mud, and shared a water bowl like the old friends they were.

As the sun began to set, Max rested his head on Sarah's lap during the walk home. His paws were tired, his fur was a mess, but his heart was full.

"Good boy," Sarah whispered, scratching behind his ears.

Max already couldn't wait for tomorrow.
